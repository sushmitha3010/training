About Us

For more than 150 years, the department of Posts (DoP) has been the backbone of the country's communication and has played a crucial role in the country's social economic development.It
touches the lives of Indian in many ways: delivering mails,accepting mails,accepting deposits under Small Savings Schemes, providing life insurance cover under Postal Life Insurance (PLI) and
Rural Postal Life Insurance (RPLI) and providing retail services like bill collection, sale of forms, etc. The DoP also acts as an agent for Goverment of India in discharging other services for
citizens such as Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantees Scheme (MGNRGERS) wage disbursement and old age pension payments.With more than 1,55,000 post offices,
the DoP has most widely distributed postal network in the world.
